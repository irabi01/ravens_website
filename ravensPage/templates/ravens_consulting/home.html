Management Structure

Department of Human Resources Management

Ravens Consulting human resources department meets business needs through managing the company's most valuable resources. The roles of this department includes: staff recruitment, safety, employee and labor relations, compliance training and development.

Department of Finance and Accounting

The finance and accounting department is responsible for recording and reporting the cash flow transactions of a company. This department has two divisions named the division of Finance and the division of Accounts.-

Operations Management Department

This department oversees all the consulting activities of Ravens Consulting. It has two divisions to help manage its daily activities. The division of Research which oversees the research activities of Ravens Consulting while the division of ICT is concerned with provision of all ICT related services offered by Ravens Consulting.

Marketing department

This department plays a vital role in promoting the business and mission of Ravens Consulting. It coordinates and produces all materials representing what we do at Ravens Consulting.
